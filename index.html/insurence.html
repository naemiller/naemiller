Preferred Providers Organizations (PPO)

A PPO plan is a type of insurance plan that connects with medical organizations. We are connected with service providers in the Colorado area. PPOs involve networks that are made up of contracted medical professionals and health insurance companies. Healthcare facilities and practitioners, known as preferred providers, offer services to the insurer's plan policyholders at reduced rates. Plan participants receive the maximum PPO benefit when they visit in-network healthcare professionals and are also offered coverage when they see out-of-network providers.
